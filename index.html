Sessile drop contact angle measurer based on extended Generalized Hough Transform (GHT)

Design of a new algorithm to automate the calculation of contact angle based on the extended Generalized Hough transform to characterize the sessile drop profile. Bashforth-Adam equation is used for fitting the profile. The results show balanced performance between accuracy and efficiency.

Look into EDM with simulation

with Prof. Jingyu Pei in Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Electric Discharging Machining (EDM) process is a precise method of manufacturing. While it is widely applied in industry, the mechanism of discharging model is still unknown, and accurate prediction of tool shaping is in great need to optimize machining parameters.

Simulation is used in this work to validate the discharging model and predict electrode shapes. Two models based on geometry and electric field are both tested. The results show accordance with the experiments, while the electric field model performs better in reducing errors.
